I'm a PhD student at Arizona State University, advised by Dr.Paulo Shakarian at ASU. I am interested in reasoning about an agent's actions in a temporal geospatial setting. My long-term research goal is to incorparate develop neurosymbolic approches for robust explainable AI that go beyond interpretability by guiding the agent toward an optimal course. My reasrch is supported by ONR, IARPA . I've also collaborated with EpochGeo, Leidos.

Prior to starting my PhD, I completed Integrated Masters of Technology in Information Communication Technology at the School of Engineering, Central University of Karnataka , with 2 gold medals. I worked as a research assitant at Lab1055,IITH as a part of Prof.Vineeth N Balasubramanian's research group. Also, I worked on a research project in medical imaging to build a brain atlas under Prof.Jayanthi Sivaswamy at IIITH.

I completed my Bachelors in Electronics and Communications Enginnering, with a gold medal. I pursued my research in Speech Emotion Recognition, advised by Prof.Dr.Anil Kumar at IIIT Hyderabad. I was fortunate enough to intern at Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL: a prestigious telecommunications service in India), Advanced Training Institute for Electronics and Process Instrumentation (ATI-EPI: of Gov. of India), and Develearn technologies as a data science research intern.

I like science fiction, travelling, origami, making art, badminton, chess, constructive debates.
